PHILOSOPHY
One picture can be better than a lot of words. The visual design exactly suggests significance of the point because it is one of visual ideas through which I can send messages and communicate with audiences efficiently. Expressing ideas with symbols and icons is an effective communication method, and it is more impressive and simpler than just words. In other words, designers are often mediators by expressing concepts in visual sense.

Graphic design has a crucial power to communicate with audiences. I realized that there are a lot of graphics that affect to audiences on advertisements and websites while I was working at iHeartMedia,Inc. Graphic designers need to grab audience’s attention by creating images in an efficient and creative way among all those many informational images that they are given. My strength is these active interactions with audiences and creative visual communications to express a concept.

My portfolio demonstrates how I used these potentials to explore creative and efficient design expressions for consumers. Purposeful color scheme and elements are used to make visual communications. Various materials are also designed to articulate with ideas because the mediums such as fabrics, computer programs, and papers are one of important factors to express a theme. The portfolio proves that I have learned understanding one phenomenon from various aspects. These works are strongly involved with conceptual, aesthetic, technical and community-based experiences in visual design. These potentials will contribute to the society through a career of visual design.

Creativity is not something that just comes up suddenly. It forms from diverse experiences and insights about the experiences. All of the works gave me wider and diverse insights and point of views. Design is not just about creating aesthetic objects, but also expressing numerous ideas about humanity and the process of living in order to find usable solutions. Design can be very flexible from some restrictions sometimes, so various perspectives along with understanding user’s situations are critical. Designers should not be stuck in one point of view, but accept different valuable insights to improve their designs. Paul Rand said that “Everything is design.” What I am living in my life means I confront and get over a lot of problems like I face and solve problems in design.
